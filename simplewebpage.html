"The Alchemist" by Paulo Coelho A timeless tale of self-discovery and dreams, The Alchemist follows Santiago, a young shepherd, on his quest to find a hidden treasure. Along the way, he learns that true wealth lies not in gold, but in following one's heart and destiny.

"To Kill a Mockingbird" by Harper Lee Set in the racially charged American South, this classic novel explores themes of justice, empathy, and moral growth through the eyes of young Scout Finch. It's a powerful reminder of the importance of compassion and courage in the face of prejudice.

"Atomic Habits" by James Clear A modern guide to personal growth, Atomic Habits reveals how small, consistent changes can lead to remarkable results. James Clear offers practical strategies for breaking bad habits, building good ones, and transforming your life one step at a time.
